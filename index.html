CBIT MUN 2018
31st August - 2nd September

LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Honorable prospective delegates, Esteemed Advisors and my dear friends,

It is an absolute privilege to welcome you to the seventh edition of CBIT Model United Nations Conference, organized in collaboration with the United Nations Information Center for India and Bhutan. To my elation, I will be your courteous host and promise to deliver an experience worth treasuring.

After six successful conferences which hosted the finest delegates from various parts of the world, we have carved a niche for ourselves as the best conference in the country. CBITMUN has always strived to provide an enriching experience to the delegates whilst professing the highest quality of debate. But we aren't just about the conference experience; our goal lies in the very Principles of the United Nations which is to promote social progress.

Being the first Model United Nations Conferences in Hyderabad, it is our moral obligation to stand out as leaders to pave way for a better implementation of the MUN to bring a significant change to peoplesâ€™ lives. Through this edition, we wish to do exactly that by extending the scope of the conference from board room discussions to foreground implementation.

Looking forward to meeting you on the 31st!

About CBITMUN

Back in 2011 CBIT MUN club was started by a group of debate enthusiasts with the motto to set up, encourage and endorse Model United Nations Conferences in the city of Hyderabad. It was Model United Nations Initiative for the first time of its level in the city of Hyderabad under the Literary Club of CBIT, Communicando. As years passed it has not only succeeded in conducting a world class conference every year, with a sharp increase in the number of participants with every edition. Since its inception the club has stood for the quality of debate and hospitality it delivers during the conference.
